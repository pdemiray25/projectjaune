Simple Omelette Recipe

An easy and quick dish, perfect for any meal. This classic omelette combines beaten eggs cooked to perfection, optionally filled with your choice of cheese, vegetables, or meats.

üë©‚Äçüç≥ Instructions

  1. Beat the eggs: In a bowl, whisk the eggs with a pinch of salt and pepper until well mixed. Add 1 tablespoon of water or milk for a fluffier texture if desired.
  2. Heat the pan: Place a non-stick frying pan over medium heat and add the butter or oil.
  3. Cook the omelette: Once the butter has melted and is bubbling, pour in the eggs. Tilt the pan so the eggs coat the surface evenly.
  4. Add fillings (optional): When the eggs start to set at the edges but remain slightly runny in the middle, sprinkle your chosen fillings over one half of the omelette.
  5. Fold and serve: Gently lift one edge and fold it over the fillings. Cook for another minute, then slide the omelette onto a plate.
  6. Enjoy: Serve hot, with extra salt and pepper if needed.
