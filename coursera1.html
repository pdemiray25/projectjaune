About the Author

Hi — I’m Lily, a traveler and photographer who loves slow travel and local food. I started this blog in 2018 to collect honest travel tips and photo stories.

I focus on budget-friendly itineraries, food guides, and packing smart. I live in Paris but I’m originally from Brazil.
